Form Management

Features

  • Multi-user system where each user possesses an individual account with distinct roles and permissions.
  • Integration with Google Authenticaion.
  • Build and publish dynamic forms.
  • The ability to publish forms for an entire organization(s) or specific user(s).
  • Integrated with Form.io to make form building more user-friendly.
  • Dynamic approval workflows.
  • Dynamic roles & permissions.
  • Advanced datatables with filters, pagincation, column toggling, and smart search functionality.
  • Additionally, the platform offers seamless email notifications, password resetting, and multi-language.
