Features

  • Multi-user system where each user possesses an individual account with distinct roles and permissions.
  • Integration with Google Sheets and Google Drive, streamlining data management and document storage.
  • Leveraging the power of queue workers, the system syncs data with the Google API, ensuring real-time accuracy and efficiency.
